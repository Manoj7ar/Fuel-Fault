    Glossary
    
          
    
            
    BER
    
            
    Building Energy Rating (Ireland). Poor bands (D–G) feed building stress in the model.
    
            
    ktoe
    
            
    Kilo-tonnes of oil equivalent — national/county energy use from SEAI-style tables.
    
            
    Fuel-income share
    
            
    Modelled share of synthetic income spent on a liquid-fuel proxy (litres × €/L), not metered bills.
    
            
    HDD multiplier
    
            
    Heating-demand heuristic by county (inland/coastal). Scales the litres proxy only.
    
            
    Cliff price
    
            
    First pre-computed price ladder step where the county crosses the threshold.
    
            
    Breach price
    
            
    €/L solved numerically where modelled fuel share crosses the threshold (may differ slightly from cliff ladder).
