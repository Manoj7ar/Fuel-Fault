Fuel policy copilot

            

              Can’t find this screen? In the left sidebar, under Workspace, click AI · Gemini (sparkles icon). The API key field is directly below.
            

            

              Google Gemini answers in natural language. Each reply is grounded in live numbers from the same Fuel Fault Lines API as the dashboard (Zerve-hosted hub or your local FastAPI via the dev-server /api proxy). Your Gemini key never touches that hub.

              What each layer does
              

                
Data (Zerve / FastAPI) — County scores, national snapshot, headline insight, thresholds, and tiers at your scenario €/L. Fetched fresh when you send a chat message (summarised for the model).

                
Knowledge (Gemini) — Explains policy trade-offs, drafts briefings, and reasons about the numbers. If the data fetch fails, say so clearly instead of inventing figures.
